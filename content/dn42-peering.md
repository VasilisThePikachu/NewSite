## Peering with Illuminate [(AS4242421413)](https://dn42.g-load.eu/explorer/#/aut-num/AS4242421413)

This page provides information to peer with me on [dn42](/projects#dn42httpsdn42euhome). Please read everything here.

### Requirements:
- Good contact info.
- Latency less then 70ms to the node(s) you wish to peer with.
- Willing to use Wireguard.
    - Preferbly with a pre-shared key but not required.
- Extended next hop support.
- ROA.
